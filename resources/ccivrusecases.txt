## Contact Center IVR Data Use Cases

### 1. Real-Time Call Volume Forecasting and Staffing Analysis

**Scenario:** A workforce management analyst needs to understand call volume patterns to optimize agent scheduling. They want to analyze historical call volumes by time of day, day of week, and seasonal patterns to predict staffing requirements for the upcoming month.

**MCP Tools Used:**
- `cortex_analyst_query` — Natural language queries against call volume semantic views for pattern analysis
- `execute_query` — Retrieves granular 15-minute interval data for detailed trend analysis
- `invoke_custom_function` — Executes time-series forecasting model to predict future volumes

**Sample Interaction:**
> *"Show me average call volume by 30-minute interval for the past 12 weeks, broken down by day of week. Identify the peak hours for each day and calculate what our staffing levels should be assuming a 4-minute average handle time and 80% occupancy target."*

**Value:** Reduces overstaffing costs by 12% while maintaining service levels through data-driven scheduling.

### 2. IVR Containment Rate Optimization

**Scenario:** The self-service product owner wants to identify which IVR paths have low containment rates (customers opting out to speak with an agent) and understand where self-service improvements would have the greatest impact on call deflection.

**MCP Tools Used:**
- `cortex_analyst_query` — Analyzes containment rates by IVR menu path, customer segment, and call reason
- `execute_query` — Retrieves detailed IVR traversal data showing exact menu sequences before agent transfer
- `validate_query` — Pre-validates complex path analysis queries before execution

**Sample Interaction:**
> *"What are our IVR containment rates by menu path for the past quarter? Show me the top 10 paths where customers most frequently opt out to an agent, the average point in the menu tree where they abandon self-service, and the call reasons associated with each path."*

**Value:** Identifies specific IVR paths for redesign, improving overall containment rate from 62% to 71%, reducing agent-handled calls by 45,000 monthly.

- `explain_query` — Validates query execution plan for large date range analysis

**Sample Interaction:**
> *"Show me the week-over-week trend in average handle time for the past 8 weeks. Break this down by call disposition code and agent tenure band. Identify which specific call types have contributed most to the AHT increase and whether it's concentrated in specific agent groups."*

**Value:** Pinpoints AHT increase to a new product launch generating unfamiliar call types, enabling targeted agent training that reduces AHT by 45 seconds within two weeks.
